Moussaka

Description

Moussaka is a layered casserole dish typically made with eggplant (aubergine), minced meat (often lamb or beef), tomatoes, and a creamy béchamel sauce. The dish is seasoned with various herbs and spices such as cinnamon, oregano, and nutmeg, providing a rich and flavorful profile. The layers of eggplant and meat are baked together, creating a hearty and comforting dish that's then topped with a velvety béchamel sauce before being baked to a golden perfection.

Ingredients

  1. 2 large eggplants
  2. Olive oil (for brushing and frying)
  3. Salt and black pepper
  4. 1 pound (about 450g) ground lamb or beef
  5. 1 large onion, finely chopped
  6. 3 cloves garlic, minced
  7. 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  8. 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  9. 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  10. 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  11. 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  12. 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  13. 2 cups whole milk

Steps to make Mousakka :

  1. Brush eggplant slices with olive oil, and season with salt and black pepper.
  2. Fry or bake the eggplant slices until they are golden brown and tender.
  3. In a pan, cook the ground meat until browned.
  4. Add chopped onion and garlic; cook until softened.
  5. Stir in diced tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
  6. Simmer until the sauce thickens.
