Lasagna recipe


Description:

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  1. 9 lasagna noodles
  2. 1 pound (about 450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  3. 1 onion, finely chopped
  4. 3 cloves garlic, minced
  5. 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  6. 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  7. 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  8. 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  9. 2 teaspoons dried basil
  10. 1/2 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  11. 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  12. 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional, for heat)
  13. 1 cup ricotta cheese
  14. 1 large egg
  15. 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  16. 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  17. Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

How to Make Lasagna Step-By-Step

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade lasagna:
  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
